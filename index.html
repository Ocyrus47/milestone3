Gurkha Golf Society (UK)

Gurkha Golf Society (UK) was established in 2007.

The Gurkha Golf Society is a passionate golfing community of retired and serving Brigade of Gurkhas members and their families in the UK,
honoring the Gurkhas' legacy of bravery and service to the British Crown.
